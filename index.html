Who We Are

A heart‑driven movement for change in Uganda

Wand Charity Foundation (WCF)

Founded with the belief that every child and every elderly person deserves dignity, care, and opportunity. Our work spans communities across Uganda, focusing on providing food, shelter, education, and medical support to those who need it most.

With your help, hope becomes action. From our main office at Ovino Mall, Kampala, we coordinate outreach programs that directly uplift lives.
